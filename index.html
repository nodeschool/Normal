About NodeSchool

NodeSchool is a collection open source workshoppers (lesson modules) that teach web software skills based around node.js. The meetups we hold are the workshops where workshoppers are used a curriculum and mentors help the attendees work through the challenges.

Slingshot CoWork

Slingshot CoWork is all about community which is why they host our events.

Our goal is to meet every week at the Slingshot CoWork to get together and share our knowledge and experiences with node.js. Checkout our Meetup page to find when our next event is going to be held.
